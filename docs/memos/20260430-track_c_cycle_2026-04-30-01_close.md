## 5. Construction-first reflection (audit R36 + R40 + V2 confirmation)

The auditor R36 + R40 thesis: portfolio-construction degrees of
freedom are the dominant binding constraint, not the factor zoo. **V1
→ V2 expansion provides a clean empirical falsification test**:

- V1 mining was restricted to 33 factors. Top-1 was
  `{beta_spy_60d, vol_regime, mean_rev_sma50}` — defensive + vol
  filter + mean-reversion. Tier 2 sibling.
- V2 mining reached 61 factors (the 64-factor pool minus 3 unmet
  data dependencies). Top-1 is
  `{beta_spy_60d, mom_12_1, volume_ratio_20d}` — defensive +
  momentum + liquidity. Still Tier 2 sibling.

Both cycles converge on the same family-tuple (A,B,C) + same
construction + same defensive thesis + share `beta_spy_60d` with
RCMv1 verbatim. The V2 expansion exposed Family E
(intraday/microstructure) and Family F (short-horizon reversal) for
the first time, but **TPE archived 0 finite-objective trials in
those families** because the 21d-forward-return target combined with
monthly long-only top-N construction kills the IC of fast-decaying
reversal/microstructure alpha.

**This is empirically the construction-collapse hypothesis confirmed**:
expanding the factor zoo by ~85% (33 → 61 factors) does not change
the converged structural family of the top trial. The binding
constraint sits at the construction layer.

### 5.1 Construction degrees of freedom not yet varied
