## What this cycle proves (and what it does not)

**Proven**:

1. **Cap_aware_cross_asset DOES break NAV correlation for some mining outcomes**: 4 of 10 trials achieved pooled raw 0.66-0.70 (partial_diversifier band), down from cycle #03's 0.85-0.95 (reject band). This is the first empirical evidence that cross-asset universe expansion materially shifts realized NAV beyond what cluster_cap alone could achieve.

2. **The breaking mechanism is asymmetric factor coverage on bonds/cash**: factors that don't compute on cross-asset symbols (amihud_20d, volume-related) cause selector to default to non-equity selection on those slots → meaningfully different NAV trajectory. This is a side effect of factor-coverage thinness, not deliberate cross-asset alpha discovery.

3. **Mining objective remains stock-favoring**: 100% of top-10 archived trials use `drawup_from_252d_low` (RCMv1 anchor); 4 of 10 use both `drawup` + `amihud` (2-of-4 RCMv1 anchors). Composite IC mining structurally converges on RCMv1-character factors regardless of cross-asset universe expansion.

4. **2025 QQQ hard gate is achievable** with cross-asset basket: 8 of 10 trials pass (2025 vs_qqq > 0). Trial 8 best: +10.5% vs QQQ in 2025 with -19% max_dd vs QQQ's -22.86%. **Cap_aware_cross_asset can produce strategies that beat QQQ in even strong-equity 2025 while improving drawdown.**

5. **Drawdown improvement is real**: Cluster A trials show -16% to -18% max_dd vs cycle #03's -27% (same factors, different construction). Cluster B trials show -27% (similar to cycle #03). The drawdown improvement is purely a Cluster-A phenomenon.

**Not proven**:

1. Whether `drawup + amihud` factor-pair siblingness is a fundamental constraint of low-DD long-only design or a mining-search artifact. (Cycle #05 hypothesis: ban these two anchors and see what mining does.)

2. Whether there's a non-RCMv1-sibling factor combination at cap_aware_cross_asset that achieves both partial-diversifier NAV AND clean factor profile. Mining didn't find one in this 200-trial run.

3. Whether 4-factor composite cardinality (vs 3) would expand the search to include non-amihud diversifiers. Yaml frozen at cardinality=3 for this cycle.

4. Whether D3b regime-aware mining objective would shift the convergence away from drawup. Smoke abort gate did NOT fire (non_equity ≥ 15% on first trial), so the formal D3b escalation is not pre-committed by yaml. Operator recommendation: D3b deferred until more evidence; current verdict is "factor convergence, not objective failure".
