a clear winner — `beta_spy_60d × amihud_20d × mom_126d`, equal-ish

ceiling`.** Per the criteria's immutability contract and the

either RCMv1 (57.73%) or Candidate-2 (31.07%) to prevent the next
batch from cloning their factor-ETF concentration profile. The bar
worked as intended.

## 4. Why the watch-share gate failed

The composite picks high-zscore basket of beta + amihud + mom_126d.
Across a 17-year construction panel the top-10 names that reliably
hit those criteria include factor / leverage / industry-momentum ETFs
that ARE on the data-quality watch list — concretely:

| watch symbol | weight-day share contribution |
|--------------|-------------------------------|
| PWR          | 8.62% (single-name max)       |
| KLAC         | 6.79%                         |
| SOXL         | 6.29% (leveraged)             |
| ACGL         | 5.64%                         |
| LRCX         | 4.87%                         |
| SCHD         | 0.98%                         |
| TT           | 0.69%                         |
| (others)     | < 0.5% each                   |

Total watch-symbol weight-day share = **39.50%**, against a 30% ceiling.

The watch list flags symbols where data-quality / coverage /
adjustment-semantics is uncertain. Concentrating ~40% of weight-days
in those symbols is exactly the failure mode the criteria
deliberately precluded ("force new candidates to be more diversified
away from watch-list factor ETFs than EITHER existing candidate").

## 6. What this cycle bought us (for the file, not for paper)

- Confirmed that the post-fix **weighted thin-data gate** (M12 audit)
  works as intended on a fresh panel: nominee binary share would be
  ~38.7% (legacy diagnostic), but weighted share = 7.51% — i.e. the
  panel-side thin coverage is mild on the symbols this composite
  selects, not a mining-time pathology.
- Confirmed the **panel-cutoff-strict (G4 → 2023-12-31)** mining
  contract executes cleanly with `--end-date` + `--drop-symbols`
  enforced from the CLI rather than from `universe.yaml` mutation
  (which remains frozen).
- Surfaced a **realized-β anomaly**: a composite naming
  `beta_spy_60d` does not yield a low-β portfolio if the convention
  is "higher zscore = top of basket". This is a feature-sign
  convention question that should be answered before the next cycle.
- Confirmed the **watch-share ceiling of 0.30 is meaningful**: this
  candidate (which would otherwise have looked acceptable on most
  axes) lands at 39.50%. Without the explicit gate it would have
  been promoted to S1 stockpile.
